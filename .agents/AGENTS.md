# Workspace Rules

The following rules apply to all tasks in this workspace:

1. **UI and Styling**: Always follow the themes and colors listed in `context/design patterns.md`.
2. **Best Practices and Stack Guidelines**: Follow the best practices, strict regulations, and guidelines to prevent future bugs as defined in `context/best-practices.md`. This includes following guidelines for the current stack.
3. **Project Specifications**: Always keep the core idea and the problem we're trying to solve in mind by referring to `context/specification.md`.
4. **Architecture Constraints**: Follow the architecture patterns outlined in `context/architecture.md` and do not stray far from them.
5. **Pre-implementation Context**: Before implementing any features, refer to the documents above for guidance to keep the project consistent.
6. **Progress Tracking**: Track your progress each time you complete a task or implement a feature by adding updates under the respective headers in `context/progress tracker.md`.
